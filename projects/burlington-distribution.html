Burlington Distribution Centre

Burlington, ON  ·  Industrial Redevelopment  ·  2022–2023

Project Overview

The Burlington Distribution Centre project involved the redevelopment of a former industrial site near the QEW into a 1.2 million square foot logistics facility. The site's prior industrial use required a Phase II ESA and risk assessment before excess soil from the excavation could be characterized and moved.

XS Soil Solutions managed the full brownfield soil program — from initial Phase II sampling through ESMP registration, soil characterization, and final Soil Lot submissions. Given the site's history, close coordination with MECP was required to confirm the re-use pathway for impacted soil destined for designated industrial receiving sites.

Services Provided

Phase II ESA and risk assessment support
ESMP for impacted and clean soil streams
340,000 m³ of brownfield soil characterized and registered
MECP liaison for impacted soil re-use pathway approval
Receiving site destination assessments (3 industrial sites)
