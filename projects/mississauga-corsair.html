Mississauga Corsair Subdivision

Mississauga, ON  ·  Residential Development  ·  2020–2022

Project Overview

The Corsair subdivision in north Mississauga involved the development of a 28-acre greenfield site into a 380-unit residential community. XS Soil Solutions provided the full QP scope from pre-construction site characterization through to final soil documentation.

Our team coordinated with the developer's civil team to integrate the soil sampling plan with grading operations, minimizing mobilization costs. All receiving sites were pre-qualified and registered well in advance of excavation, ensuring no soil movement delays during the project's critical-path earthworks phase.

Services Provided

Pre-construction site characterization (28 acres)
ESMP registration with City of Mississauga
190,000 m³ of soil tracked and registered
4 receiving site destination assessments
Mass balance reporting for project closeout
