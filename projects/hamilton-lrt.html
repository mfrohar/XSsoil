Hamilton LRT Infrastructure Corridor

Hamilton, ON  ·  Transit Infrastructure  ·  2022–2023

Project Overview

The Hamilton LRT Infrastructure Corridor required comprehensive excess soil management across a 14 km linear transit alignment traversing multiple soil conditions and land uses. XS Soil Solutions was engaged as the Qualified Person (QP) for all aspects of O. Reg. 406/19 compliance throughout the pre-construction and construction phases.

Our team developed and registered an Excess Soil Management Plan (ESMP) that coordinated the movement of over 280,000 cubic metres of excavated material to pre-approved receiving sites across the Greater Hamilton Area. Soil characterization sampling was completed at 47 discrete sampling locations along the corridor, with all Soil Lot registrations submitted to the provincial registry within the required 48-hour window.

Services Provided

Excess Soil Management Plan (ESMP) — registered with MECP
Soil characterization at 47 sampling locations
Soil Lot registrations for 280,000+ m³ of material
Receiving site destination assessments (7 sites)
On-site QP supervision during excavation phases
