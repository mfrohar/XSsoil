Why Join Us

What we offer

Professional Growth

We support P.Eng. and P.Geo. designation pathways for all eligible staff, covering exam fees, study materials, and providing the supervised experience hours required for licensure.

Collaborative Culture

Small enough that your work has real impact, large enough to have the project diversity that keeps your skills sharp. Senior QPs mentor junior staff on every project, not just at performance review time.

Competitive Compensation

Competitive base salary benchmarked against Ontario consulting industry standards, group health and dental benefits, RRSP matching, mileage reimbursement, and paid professional development days.

Flexible Arrangements

Hybrid work arrangements for office-based roles, flexible start/finish times, and an understanding that field schedules sometimes require early mornings and early afternoons off.

Meaningful Work

Excess soil management directly protects Ontario's land and water resources. Our work matters to the environment and to the communities where we live and work.

Diverse Project Experience

Work on residential subdivisions, municipal infrastructure, commercial developments, and industrial redevelopments across Ontario — no two projects are the same.
