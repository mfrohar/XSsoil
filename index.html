O.Reg. 406/19

Onsite and Excess Soil Regulations

The province of Ontario estimates that 25 million cubic metres of excess soil is excavated annually. While most soil can be reused safely, it can occasionally contain contaminants that affect surface and groundwater quality. In response, new rules are being phased in from 2021 to 2025.
