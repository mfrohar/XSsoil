Regulatory History

Ontario's excess soil regulation timeline

2021
O. Reg. 406/19 Takes Effect
Ontario's On-Site and Excess Soil Management regulation officially came into force, replacing the old Interim Guidelines with legally binding requirements for project leaders.
2022
Phase 2: ESMP Mandatory for Class 1
Excess Soil Management Plans became mandatory for Class 1 projects. QP oversight requirements expanded, requiring full characterization and registration for all major projects.
2023
Registry Full Rollout
The Ontario Excess Soil Registry (ECA) fully operational. All regulated projects required to register soil lots, receiving sites, and track movements from source to destination.
2025
Enhanced Enforcement
MECP increased compliance audit frequency and penalty thresholds. Proactive documentation and QP oversight now more critical than ever for project teams across Ontario.
