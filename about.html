About Us Who We Are

Built for the complexity of Ontario's regulatory landscape

"We started XS Soil Solutions because we saw too many good projects derailed by regulatory surprises that could have been prevented with the right expertise applied early."

XS Soil Solutions was founded in Burlington, Ontario with a singular focus: providing Ontario's construction industry with expert-level excess soil management services delivered by professionals who understand both the regulatory framework and the practical realities of construction timelines and budgets.

When O. Reg. 406/19 came into force in 2021, many project leaders found themselves navigating unfamiliar regulatory terrain. XS Soil Solutions was purpose-built to fill this gap — assembling a team of Qualified Persons with deep experience in environmental site assessment, soil science, and Ontario's evolving regulatory environment.

Today, we serve constructors, land developers, property owners, and municipalities across Ontario from our Burlington headquarters. Every engagement is staffed by a licensed QP from day one — because in this field, experience and credentials are not optional. We believe that the right professional guidance, applied proactively, pays for itself many times over in avoided delays, reduced disposal costs, and regulatory certainty.

What Drives Us

Our core values

01

Compliance First

Every recommendation we make prioritizes regulatory compliance above all else. We will never suggest a shortcut that puts your project or our professional credentials at risk. Our clients trust us because they know we will always give them the honest regulatory assessment, even when it is not what they hoped to hear.

02

Cost Efficiency

Regulatory compliance does not have to be expensive. We bring creativity and commercial awareness to every engagement, identifying soil reuse opportunities, optimizing sampling programs, and delivering fixed-fee proposals that give our clients budget certainty from the start. We measure our value in dollars saved, not just documents delivered.

03

On-Schedule Delivery

We understand that in construction, time is money. Our team is structured to meet aggressive deadlines: QPs who respond same day, field technicians who mobilize within 24–48 hours, and report preparation workflows that prioritize speed without sacrificing quality. We treat your schedule as if it were our own.
