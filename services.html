Services What We Do

Comprehensive soil management services

Every service you need to achieve O. Reg. 406/19 compliance, from initial characterization through final sign-off.

Excess Soil Management Plans

The cornerstone compliance document for any project generating excess soil in Ontario. Our Qualified Persons prepare compliant ESMPs, register soil lots in the Ontario Excess Soil Registry, and manage all documentation from initial site assessment through final project sign-off.

O. Reg. 406/19 Class 1 Projects QP Authored Registry Filing

Site Alteration Plans

Required by most Ontario municipalities before significant grading, fill placement, or soil removal can begin. We prepare SAPs tailored to each jurisdiction's specific by-law requirements — eliminating the revision cycles that cause costly project delays.

Municipal Permits Drainage Assessment ESC Plan

Soil Characterization Reports

The analytical report that determines whether excess soil meets quality standards for reuse at receiving sites. We coordinate field sampling, accredited lab analysis, and Table 1/2 comparisons — all required for soil lot registration in the Ontario Excess Soil Registry.

Table 1 & 2 Lab Analysis Registry-Ready
Request a Quote

Excess Soil Destination Assessments

Required for receiving sites before they can accept excess soil under O. Reg. 406/19. Our QPs assess the baseline environmental condition of the receiving site, prepare the ESDAR, and register it in the Registry — protecting site owners from future environmental liability.

Receiving Sites Baseline Assessment Registry Filing

Fill Management Plans

For sites accepting significant volumes of incoming fill, our Fill Management Plans establish truck-by-truck documentation, pre-acceptance testing protocols, placement mapping, and inspection schedules — giving site owners confidence that every load meets quality standards.

Soil Management Systems
